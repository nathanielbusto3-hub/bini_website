Who We Are?

The Nation's Girl Group

BINI is a Filipino girl group formed by Star Magic and managed by ABS-CBN. The group consists of eight talented members: Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena. Their name "BINI" (pronounced "bee-nee") means "blessed" in Filipino.

Since their debut in 2021, BINI has taken the P-Pop industry by storm with their captivating performances, powerful vocals, and infectious energy. They have become one of the most influential girl groups in the Philippines, inspiring millions of fans known as "Bloom."

With hit songs like "Salamin, Salamin," "Pantropiko," and "Karera," BINI continues to break records and push the boundaries of Filipino pop music on both local and international stages.
